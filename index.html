About Me

Where creativity meets
computation.

I'm pursuing a B.S. in Artificial Intelligence and Computational Biology at Sri Sathya Sai Institute of Higher Learning, Puttaparthi — exploring the crossroads of machine learning, bioinformatics, and healthcare technology.

Alongside my studies, I'm building a strong creative profile through video & photo editing, motion design, and short-form content creation. I love turning raw footage, ideas, and brand stories into visuals that feel clean, intentional, and worth watching twice.

I care about detail — the rhythm of a cut, the softness of a shadow, the confidence of a typographic hierarchy. Whether I'm training a model or grading a reel, my goal is the same: make something useful, and make it look right.
